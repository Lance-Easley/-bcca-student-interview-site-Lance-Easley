Base Camp Coding Academy Student Interview

Seth Hogue's Portrait

My name is Lance Easley. I'm a student at BCCA where I'm training to become a full stack software developer. Our assignment for today, November 19, 2020, was to interview our table partner and make a website out of the information we gathered. I interviewed Seth Hogue, my table partner at the time.

I asked Seth these three questions:

  • What has been your toughest week at BCCA?
  • What has been the easiest week at BCCA?
  • What is your biggest victory so far at BCCA?

Seth responded respectively:

  • The hardest week for me was probably either when we started working with for/while loops or the first unit project week.
  • The easiest was probably the first or second week, I was picking up on Python pretty quickly, so it wasn't too difficult for me starting off.
  • My biggest victory was probably creating a working GUI for my Pokemon Rock, Paper, Scissors program that I made for the first unit project.
